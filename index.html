How We Work

Precision at Every Step

01
Yarn Sourcing & Testing

Every order begins with certified yarn — GOTS-approved organic cotton or compliance-verified blends — tested for tensile strength, colour fastness, and shrinkage before production.

Yarn approval rate: 98.5%
02
Knitting & Fabric Construction

State-of-the-art circular knitting machines produce fabric to exact GSM specifications. Our composite vertical integration means we control every loop of every garment.

Defect rate: < 0.5%
03
Cutting & Stitching

Precision die-cutting followed by skilled stitching — lock stitch, overlock, and flatlock techniques applied by certified operators under continuous quality supervision.

On-spec rate: 99.2%
04
Washing, Finishing & QC

Garments undergo bio-washing, enzyme treatment, and pressing — then face a 47-point quality control inspection aligned with each buyer's CoC requirements.

First-pass QC: 97.8%
05
Packing & Export

Custom packing per buyer spec — pre-packs, retail hangers, or bulk. FOB Karachi dispatch with full documentation for seamless customs clearance worldwide.

Buyer FAQ

Everything You Need to Know Before Ordering

Answers to the 16 most common questions from new and existing buyers — covering ordering, compliance, quality, logistics, and pricing.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Our standard MOQ is 1,000 pieces per style per colour. For programmes exceeding 5,000 pieces we offer tiered pricing. Sample orders of 3–5 pieces per style are available before bulk commitment — sampling costs are deducted from the first bulk order.
What is the typical lead time for a bulk order?
Standard lead time is 60–90 days from order confirmation and advance payment, depending on fabric availability and embellishment complexity. Rush programmes of 45 days are possible for certain styles at a premium. We work backwards from your in-store date to set the production schedule.
How does the sampling process work?
Sampling proceeds in three stages: Proto Sample (from your tech pack or reference, 7–10 days), Fit Sample (after your corrections, 7 days), and Pre-Production Sample (from bulk fabric, 10–14 days). PP sample approval triggers bulk production. Sampling fee is typically $15–40 per style depending on complexity.
Do you work with buyer-nominated fabrics or trims?
Yes. We can work with buyer-nominated yarn, fabric, labels, hangtags, and packaging. Buyers are responsible for timely delivery of nominated materials to our factory. Alternatively, we source from approved suppliers against your specifications — often at better cost due to our volume relationships.
What quality control process do you follow?
We operate a 47-point inline and final QC system aligned with AQL 2.5 (Level II). Key checkpoints: yarn lab tests, fabric inspection (4-point system), in-line stitching audits, end-line inspection, and final random inspection before packing. Third-party inspections (SGS, Bureau Veritas, Intertek) are accommodated on request.
Can you match Pantone colours accurately?
Yes. We use spectrophotometer-based colour matching with a Delta E tolerance of ≤1.0 for most shades. Lab dip approvals are provided digitally (spectral data + physical swatch). Some fluorescent or specialty colours may require wider tolerances — we advise on a per-shade basis.
What wash testing and care label standards do you apply?
We test to ISO 105 (colour fastness to washing, rubbing, light, perspiration) as standard. Care labels follow ISO 3758 GINETEX symbols or ASTM D5489 (US market). Test reports from accredited labs (SGS / Intertek) are provided per bulk order. Additional tests (pilling, abrasion, seam strength) available on request.
Which compliance certifications does Kings Apparel hold?
Current active certifications include: GOTS, SA 8000, ISO 9001:2015, WRAP, BSCI, SEDEX, OEKO-TEX Standard 100, REACH, FSC, and multiple buyer-specific Codes of Conduct. All audit reports and certificates are available digitally on request, or accessible via SEDEX/BSCI portals.
Can you produce organic cotton garments?
Yes — we are GOTS certified. Our organic range covers all categories from newborn to adult. We source 100% GOTS-certified yarn from approved suppliers and maintain full chain-of-custody documentation. Transaction certificates are issued per shipment for end-to-end traceability.
Are factory audits available to buyers?
Absolutely. We welcome on-site factory audits and virtual audit calls. Please contact us to schedule. For SEDEX/BSCI members, our shared audit reports are accessible through those platforms. We maintain an open-door policy — our confidence comes from having nothing to hide.
What shipping terms do you offer?
We primarily ship FOB Port Qasim / Karachi. CIF to major European, US, Australian, and Middle Eastern ports can be arranged. We work with DHL, Maersk, MSC, and established freight forwarders. Ex-Works pricing is available for buyers with their own logistics arrangements.
What packing and labelling options are available?
We offer flat-pack, retail-ready hanger packing, polybag per piece, and bulk carton options. Custom inner boxes, tissue paper, hangtags, and barcode labels are all available. Packing specifications are followed precisely as per buyer's packing manual — deviations trigger re-packing at our cost.
What are your payment terms?
Standard terms are 30% advance T/T against order confirmation, balance 70% against shipping documents or Letter of Credit at sight. For established buyers with a track record, extended net-30 or net-60 terms may be available after credit evaluation. We also accept LC usance.
Do prices include compliance documentation costs?
Standard compliance documents (GOTS TC, test reports, CoO, invoice, packing list) are included in the FOB price. Additional third-party inspection fees (SGS, Bureau Veritas etc.) are charged at cost. Buyer-specific lab testing beyond our standard suite is quoted separately based on the test suite required.
